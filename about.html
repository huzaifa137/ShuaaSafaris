About Us

Shuaa Safaris Limited

Shuaa Safaris Limited was incorporated on the 21st / July/2008 as a private limited company specializing in provision of customized tours, car hire, hotel and conference reservations, safaris, event management, travel Visas and haulage. We arrange client packages, advice on trips highlighting bio-diversity and Geo tourism to all East and Central Africa National parks, forest reserves, in our fleet of Minibuses, Land cruisers, Saden cars.

  • Car-hire, hotel reservations and visa When you penetrate the thick and dense forests of Bwindi you will set your eyes on one of the world’s most endangered species, the mountain gorilla. These amazing creatures are also referred to as man’s closest relative. Away from Bwindi, a visit to Kibale, provocative shrieks of monkeys and darting forest birds fill the air while you follow the steps of a range. Colobus Monkeys jump from branch to branch, acrobatic mangabeys swing high in the canopy and as all this in progress chimpanzees are closely watching.
